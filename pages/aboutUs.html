Swagatam

We at Khukuri Nepalese Restaurant are committed in preserving a culture, tradition and standard in culinary tastes you would appreciate. It has been more than ten years since Khukuri Nepalese restaurant established itself in Irving TX. And has since become an icon of Nepalese/Indian cuisine among locals and visitors alike.

Our Approach

If you are a first-time visitor, you will find our cuisine versatile, ranging from extremely mild to richly spicy. We offer a richly-varied menu of fresh, natural ingredients, wholesome sauces and flavourful spices. All of our food is natural and freshly-prepared daily on the premises with no additives or preservatives

Methodology

One of Irving's well known Nepalese restaurant, Khukuri Nepalese has gained a reputation for it's traditional yet creative cuisine. The food of Nepal is as diverse as the country itself. Welcome to the world of DAAL (Lentils), BHAAT (Rice) and TARKARI (Curry) and a little bit of everything in between!
